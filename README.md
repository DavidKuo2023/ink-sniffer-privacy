Inksniffer Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Inksniffer is a personal library management app. We respect and protect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera Access
Used to scan book barcodes and QR codes for adding books to your library. We do not capture, store, or upload any photos or videos.

1.2 Photo Library Access
Used to select book cover images. We only access photos you actively choose and never read other content in your library.

1.3 In-App Purchase Information
When you purchase "Pro Upgrade," Apple processes all payments. We only receive a purchase receipt to unlock features. We do not store your payment card information or Apple account details.

1.4 Locally Stored Data
Book information, shelf data, and reading records are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

1.5 Anonymous Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage statistics (such as feature usage frequency) to improve the product experience. This data is not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used only to:
• Provide and maintain app functionality
• Improve user experience
• Process in-app purchase orders

3. Information Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. Since book data is stored locally, we recommend regular backups using the export feature.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:
• Apple In-App Purchase: for processing purchases
• Douban API: for fetching book rating information
These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be notified within the app or on this page.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact: inksniffer@hotmail.com
